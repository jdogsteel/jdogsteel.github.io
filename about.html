ABOUT

I think that if a game is designed well then it can change someone's life. to achieve this goal I make a conscious effort to keep my design centered around the player and their emotions, goals, and desires. If a game is centered around the player from the beginning of development, then the game is more likely to resonate with its users. Having the systems understanding I do allows me to create logical systems, storyarchs, and reusable code. I am passionate about the experience that the player will have, but more importantly I am passionate about how to achieve thos experiences.
